Put it in its own folder. On first startup, the password you use is the password that will be set for the image locker. To add an image, you have to copy it to your clipboard and click "Paste Image." To view an image, you just have to click on it. To edit its tags, remove it, or copy it to your clipboard, right click the image in the main list or the expanded view. Separate tags with a space. You can then enter those tags into the search box and hit enter to search for images with the given tags. To clear the search terms, just clear the search box and hit enter in it.
